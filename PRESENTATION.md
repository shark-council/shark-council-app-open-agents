# Problem

Today’s crypto traders are drowning in data.

AI helps, but generic models drop the high-stakes signals that lead to bad trades.

Right now, fixing this requires complex prompt engineering or vibe coding.

It’s frustrating, it requires technical skills most don't have, and it leaves the majority of traders wasting time on obsolete tools.

# Solution

That’s why we built Shark Council.

Instead of wrestling with prompts, a trader simply pitches their idea and lets a council of specialized AI sharks roast it.

A trader can customize their council with whatever agents they need: technical analysts, macro experts, sentiment trackers—or even agents trained on Trump’s X posts or astrology forecasts.

Any AI developer can build and list an agent on our marketplace.

But how do we ensure these agents are actually good?

We integrated the ENS protocol to handle discovery, identity, and reputation.

Every agent gets its own ENS subdomain and a transparent track record showing its win rate, and trade history.

Finally, when the council approves a trade, we execute it directly through the Uniswap API, handling everything from entry to stop loss and take profit.

We use Uniswap's integrator fee parameters to take a cut, which we use to reward the developers of the best-performing agents.

In short: when the trader wins, the AI developer gets paid. It's a perfect, sustainable loop.

# Demo 1

Let’s see it in action.

Say you spot a bullish post about Bitcoin on X.

You drop your thesis into Shark Council.

Next, you browse ENS identities to draft your 'sharks' based on their on-chain reputation, and you hit Start the Roast.

Our Orchestrator pits these agents against each other. They analyze your idea using their specialized data streams and debate the merits.

Once the dust settles, the Orchestrator delivers a final verdict.

If the council approves, we instantly execute the trade via the Uniswap API, automatically locking in your take profit and stop loss.

On your live dashboard, you track everything: entry price, token amounts, the transaction hash, and the pending integrator fee.

When the trade hits your exit conditions, it auto-closes.

Here is the best part: if you made a profit, the winning agents earn their fee.

If the trade loses, that fee goes right back into your pocket.

The agents only get paid when you win.

# Demo 2

But a marketplace is nothing without great creators. So, let’s look at the developer side.

Listing your AI agent takes seconds. You just plug in your agent’s details, provide the API endpoint, and link the wallet where you want to receive your fees.

From there, our AI moderator takes over. It verifies the data and automatically mints a dedicated ENS subdomain, instantly establishing your agent's on-chain identity and reputation tracker.

You'll get an email as soon as you're live.

# Demo 3

It’s not just a trading app—it’s an open developer ecosystem.
